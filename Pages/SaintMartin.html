Journey to Saint Martin’s Island: A Tropical Paradise
Introduction
Saint Martin’s Island, nestled in the azure waters of the Bay of Bengal, beckons travelers with its pristine beaches, swaying palms, and captivating sunsets. As the only coral island in Bangladesh, it offers a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. In this essay, we’ll explore the route from Dhaka to this idyllic island.
1. Getting to Teknaf
Before embarking on your island adventure, you must first reach Teknaf, the gateway to Saint Martin’s Island. Here are the steps:
a. Dhaka to Cox’s Bazar
Begin your journey by taking a train from Dhaka to Cox’s Bazar. The Parjotak Express is a popular choice. Upon reaching Cox’s Bazar, hop into a taxi and head to Jetty 6 in Teknaf.
2. Choosing Your Mode of Transport
Once in Teknaf, you have two main options for reaching Saint Martin’s Island:
a. Train and Ferry (Recommended)
This route combines the comfort of a train journey with the thrill of a ferry ride. Take the train from Dhaka to Cox’s Bazar. From Cox’s Bazar, travel by taxi to Jetty 6. Finally, board the ferry that will carry you across the sparkling waters to Saint Martin’s Island. This option takes approximately 12 hours and 22 minutes and costs around $29 to $52.
b. Bus and Ferry
If you prefer a direct bus journey, take a bus from Dhaka to Cox’s Bazar. Once in Cox’s Bazar, proceed to Jetty 6 and catch the ferry to Saint Martin’s Island. This alternative takes about 13 hours and 36 minutes and costs between $44 and $52.
3. Arriving at Saint Martin’s Island
As you step off the ferry onto the powdery sands of Saint Martin’s Island, a sense of tranquility envelops you. Here’s what awaits:
a. Beach Exploration
Wander along the pristine beaches, such as the famous Chera Dwip.
Feel the soft sand beneath your feet and listen to the gentle lapping of the waves.
b. Snorkeling and Diving
Dive into the crystal-clear waters and explore the vibrant marine life. Snorkeling enthusiasts will be delighted by the colorful coral reefs.
c. Sunset Views
As evening approaches, find a quiet spot to witness the mesmerizing sunset. The sun dips below the horizon, casting hues of orange and pink across the sky.
4. Practical Considerations
Prices: Be prepared for reasonable expenses on the island.
Hotel reservation
Availability: Check ferry schedules in advance.
Accommodation: Consider staying at the Saint Martin Island Resort for a comfortable experience.
Conclusion
Saint Martin’s Island, with its natural beauty and warm hospitality, promises an unforgettable escape. Whether you choose the train-ferry combo or the bus-ferry route, the journey itself becomes part of the adventure. So pack your bags, leave behind the city’s chaos, and set sail for this tropical paradise!
