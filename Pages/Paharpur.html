Paharpur Guide

How to go there


First, you need to go to the district of Naogaon.You can travel by bus or train.Get to the zero point of the town which is called Muktir mor. From there, you can hire a suitable vehicle to reach Badalgachi sub-district.From there, use rickshaw or CNG to reach Paharpur. For convinience, check bus and train schedules in advance.
